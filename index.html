Shrikrushna Gokuldhan Gouseva Goushala, Solapur.

The Gaushala, situated in Waralegaon, South Solapur, was established in the year 2022. It encompasses five cow sheds, with a combined capacity to accommodate 100 cattle. Presently, the Gaushala houses a total of 50 cattle, comprising 30 cows, 5 bulls, and 15 calves. Additionally, the premises include approximately 0.5 acres of land cultivated with lush green grass.

Our location and area

Our trust, dedicated to the protection and welfare of cows, is nestled in the heart of Solapur, Maharashtra. Situated amidst the serene landscapes of Solapur, our trust stands as a beacon of compassion and care for these gentle creatures. We strive to provide a safe haven where cows are revered and protected, embodying the rich cultural heritage of our region. Together, with the support of our community, we work tirelessly to ensure the well-being and dignity of every cow entrusted to our care.

How we protect cows.

At our trust, our primary focus is on the protection and welfare of cows. We provide a safe haven where these gentle creatures are sheltered, nurtured, and cared for. Our dedicated team ensures that each cow receives the attention and support they need to live a life free from harm and neglect. We believe in the inherent value of every cow and are committed to ensuring their well-being, embodying the spirit of compassion and respect for all living beings.

Side products.

At our trust, we prioritize the well-being of cows, providing shelter, healthcare, and collecting side products like milk. We ensure that each cow receives care and respect while exploring innovative uses for by-products such as cow dung, promoting sustainability and ethical stewardship of these animals.
